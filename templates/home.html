About the Web Application

Our Pollution Monitoring System is designed to help individuals, organizations, and governments track and manage pollution levels in their environment. By leveraging the power of data analysis, the system provides real-time insights into air quality. Users can manually input data or analyze pre-existing datasets to assess pollution levels and take proactive measures to protect our planet.

Key Features:

  • Real-time pollution tracking and analysis using advanced algorithms
  • User-friendly interface to input and analyze environmental data
  • Real-time feedback on air pollution levels
  • Insights into pollution categories such as Very Poor, Poor, Average, Good, and Very Good
  • Interactive visualizations and reports for better decision-making
